North Wales Charity Ramblers

For many years a dedicated group of friends from the Penyffordd area have raised thousands of pounds for the benefit of local charities. Click Here to see what the local press have said about us in the past.

Every year we organise, and take part in, a variety of sponsored events, including walks, cycle rides and horse rides.

During 2018 we will be raising money for Marie Curie Cancer Care

We need your help !

Any donation, however large or small (by way of sponsorship or raffle prizes), will be greatly appreciated. This year we will again be holding an auction for items the "money can't buy" and authenticated or autographed personal items would be much appreciated.

Alternatively anybody wishing to take part or help out in any way would be most welcome.

We hope this web site will tell you more about our activities, and that you will consider supporting us in some way.
