The West Highland Way Revisited

May 2002

 

In May 2002 we again walked the West Highland Way, this time with a much larger group. The size of the group made logistics much more difficult and we stayed in three "centres", Drymen, Tyndrum and Fort William. We used two minibuses to take us to the start of each day, and pick us up at the end.
