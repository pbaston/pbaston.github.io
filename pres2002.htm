2002 Presentation

 

This write-up appeared in the Chester Chronicle shortly after the 2002 Presentation.

 

 Every year a grand presentation night is held at Penyffordd British Legion. This picture shows the cheque for £7200 being presented to the Friends of Ysgol Plas Brondyffryn.

Having done a lot of walking in Scotland Chris made good his promise to wear a kilt for the presentation ! It was generally agreed that it suited him, despite much speculation about what he was wearing underneath !
