The Wicklow Way

Sept 2001

 

During September 2001 we walked the Wicklow Way long distance footpath in Ireland. This 76 mile path starts in Marlay Park, Co Dublin, and finishes in Clonegal, Co Carlow.

From Marlay Park the Way ascends into the Dublin Mountains. Superb views across the City of Dublin !
Bob by the J.B. Malone memorial, above Lough Tay. This stone is a tribute to one of the main pioneers of the Wicklow Way.
Dave & Cath, with Lough Tay in the background.
The beautiful Glendalough - valley of the two lakes.
Glendalough was our base for the walk.
Cooling our feet, near Tinahely.
"The Dying Cow", the wonderful Tallon's pub, near Shillelagh.
The end of the Way ! Entering the village of Clonegal.
